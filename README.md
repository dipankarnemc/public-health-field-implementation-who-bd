**WHO Bangladesh – NCD and Mental Health Program Work Summary (2022–2025)**

This repository presents a collection of materials, tools, and documentation reflecting my work with the World Health Organization (WHO) Bangladesh under the NCD and Mental Health program. I worked mainly in Jashore and Jhenaidah districts to strengthen primary health care service delivery and support national efforts in risk factor prevention, community engagement, and health promotion.

The repository includes activity summaries, tools I developed, training contributions, and documents prepared during my tenure. All content is shared responsibly, without including confidential or sensitive program data.

**Key Contributions**
**Strengthening NCD and Mental Health Services**

Supported the rollout of WHO PEN and mhGAP in PHC settings.

Conducted supervision visits, follow-up support, and quality monitoring at UHCs and tele-mental health centres.

Facilitated mhGAP sessions, including data reporting and Essential Care modules.

Supported development and adaptation of implementation materials.

**Surveillance and Data Strengthening**

Developed a mental health sentinel surveillance data collection tool for Chapai Nawabganj, Sylhet, and Jashore.

Facilitated training on data collection and data protection in Manirampur UHC, Jashore, Bangladesh.

**Community Engagement and Awareness**

Participated in community awareness sessions on suicide prevention in Jashore and Jhenaidah.

Joined school-based awareness activities in three schools across both districts.

Contributed to events for World No Tobacco Day, World Mental Health Day, and other health observances.

**Health Promotion in Settings**

Promoted school, workplace, and city-based health initiatives.

Encouraged practices like avoiding plastic water bottles in meetings and using health-friendly event setups.
