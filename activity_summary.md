# Activity Summaries  
This document provides a consolidated overview of the key activities carried out under the NCD and Mental Health program in Jashore and Jhenaidah.

## Strengthening NCD and Mental Health Services
- Coordinated regularly with DGHS, Civil Surgeon offices, and UHFPOs to support the implementation of activities under the NCD and Mental Health workplan.
- Supported the establishment and follow-up of tele-mental health centres in six UHCs in Jashore and initiated discussions for replication in Jhenaidah.
- Facilitated five mhGAP training sessions and delivered sessions on data reporting and Essential Care.
- Provided supportive supervision to mhGAP-trained providers in collaboration with Moner Bondhu.
- Conducted field-level observations to identify service delivery gaps and inform follow-up planning.

## Surveillance and Data Strengthening
- Developed a sentinel mental health surveillance data collection tool used in Chapainawabganj, Sylhet, and Jashore.
- Conducted training on data collection and data protection at Manirampur UHC.
- Supported monthly data collection and reporting using DGHS and WHO formats.

## Health Promotion and Community Engagement
- Supported district-level events for World No Tobacco Day, World Hypertension Day, and other health observances.
- Promoted WHO Healthy Workplace practices, including the shift from plastic water bottles to reusable alternatives.
- Participated in community awareness sessions on suicide prevention.
- Joined school-based mental health sessions in three schools across Jashore and Jhenaidah.
